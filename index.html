Engagement Party

Typically hosted by either the parents of the bride or the parents of the groom, this party should take place within one to three months of the engagement.

Bridal Shower or Couples' Shower

Hosted closer to the wedding date-usually around two months to two weeks prior-the bridal shower celebrates the bride and showers her with gifts.

Welcome Party

For couples hosting a weekend-long wedding, especially a destination wedding, kicking off the weekend's festivities starts with a welcome party.
